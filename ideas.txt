Timer, length of song. When it runs out, you die. Must make it through the levels before that.

3 levels, different colour themes.

Enemies, hexagons come at you.

Some enemies shoot, some kemikaze.

Print "Hurry up!" or "You're doing well!" aso on screen depending on player position and time left.

Multiplier when killing enemies, x2, x3, x4 aso that gives you more points when moving forward after having killed an enemy. Lasts for a while, stacks when killing more.

Sound on bullet impact.
